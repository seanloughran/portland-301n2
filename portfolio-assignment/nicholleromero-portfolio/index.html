Nicholle M. Romero

Nicholle M. Romero

Fueled by passion, drive, and ambition to create innovative work in the tech industry.

I received an undergraduate degree in Finance with minors in Mathematics and Physics from the University of Denver. As a student in the honors college, I wrote my thesis on an analysis of the public sector and private sector's role in eradicating global poverty. After completing my undergraduate degree, I immediately traveled the world on my own, following up on my thesis topic, volunteering and working to solve global poverty. I enrolled in graduate school the following year and received my International MBA from the University of Denver in 2015. I continued my pursuit of international sustainable development throughout this program while completing several consulting projects including work for Deutsche Bank's Impact Investment Fund and Nokero. These projects involved on-site research in South America and Africa.

Since finishing my graduate degree, I have worked as an Investment Analyst for RVK, Inc., developing my analytical and quantitative skills.
