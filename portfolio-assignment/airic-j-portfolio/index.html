Nice to meet you!

I'm passionate about building meaningful tools and experiences for people, communities and businesses. I've been building websites for five years and in spring 2016 I made the leap to transition into a full-time web developer career through Code Fellows. Before that I spent four years at Airbnb managing projects on the Product team and as an analyst on the Sales team.

I graduated from Claremont McKenna College in 2012 with a dual degree in Economics and Psychology but found my passion was web startups. I maintain a carpool community website ("Casual Carpool") that helps thousands of carpoolers in the San Francisco Bay Area get to work quickly and cheaply. I hope to help scale this community one day and also start a for-profit web business of my own. My current focus is to hone my development skills and help to create product experience for early-stage web businesses.
